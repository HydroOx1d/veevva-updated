FAQ

What does it take to become a creator?
A public social media profile High-quality, shoppable content posted regularly
Is there a minimum number of social media followers required to become a creator?
No, Veevva gives you the ability to sell products with any number of followers.
How do I add a product to Veevva’s catalog?
I. Log in as a creator
II. Go to the “Add product” page
III. Choose products based on categories, product price, and % you earn
IV. Add them to your personal catalog
Do I have to have my own product?
No, you sell products provided by Veevva only
How much will I earn from each product I sell?
You earn a % of each product you sell. You will see the amount of earnings when you add an item to the catalog.
Who handles shipping, customer service, returns?
Veevva handles shipping, returns and customer service.
Where do I start?
I. Sign up as a creator:
Provide your social media accounts and get approved!

II. Create your own store:
Drag and drop our products to your personal catalog to begin. Choose from hundreds of cool, unique products and brands on our platform.

III. Share the link for your store catalog on your social media accounts:
Instagram, Facebook, YouTube, TikTok, or Twitter and start selling the products and earning your rewards.
